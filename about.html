Safety

Safety is our Priority

All of our coats, jackets, and leashes were crafted to be extra-safe for your pup. They were tested vigorously to ensure the highest of safety standards were met before leaving our warehouse and reaching your dog.

We Stand by our Quality

Our mission is to provide comfortable, luxurious coats and jackets for your four-legged best friend at reasonable prices. At Posh Pups, we believe quality products do not have to be expensive. We offer high-quality products for a fraction of the price of our competitors.
